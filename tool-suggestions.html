Tools Suggestion

This page provides information about usefull tools designed to assist in analyzing market sentiment and predicting price movements.

  • Tradingview.com Compares current and historical P/E ratios, analyzes trends, and compares to industry peers.
  • FinViz.com Compares actual vs. expected EPS, analyzes growth trends, and evaluates sustainability.
  • Yahoo Finance Tracks changes in shares outstanding and their impact on EPS.
  • MacroTrends.net Stock Screener, Stock Research, Market indexes, Precious Metals, Energy, Commodities, Interest Rates, Economy, Global Metrics.
